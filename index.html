WELCOME TO NMAM Istitute of Technology

NMAM Institute of Technology (NMAMIT), located in Nitte, Karnataka, is a prestigious engineering college established in 1986. It is part of the Nitte Education Trust and offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various branches of engineering, computer science, and management. The college is affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belagavi.

NMAMIT is well-known for its high academic standards, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and a strong emphasis on research and development. It boasts a highly qualified faculty and offers a student-centric learning environment. The college is recognized for its industry-oriented curriculum, and students frequently engage in internships and industry projects. The campus is equipped with modern amenities, including libraries, laboratories, sports facilities, and hostels.

Apart from academics, NMAMIT encourages co-curricular and extracurricular activities, including technical fests, cultural events, and sports competitions. The college also emphasizes entrepreneurship, with an established incubation center for aspiring startups.

The institute has earned a reputation for producing skilled graduates who are well-equipped for the challenges of the corporate world, and it has strong placement records with ties to leading global companies. NMAMIT also actively promotes environmental sustainability and social responsibility through various initiatives.
